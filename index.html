About Us

Campus Shelf is your digital campus library built to help students explore, access, and track library resources effortlessly. Many students travel long distances only to find books unavailable or library hours closed. With Campus Shelf, you can check book availability, explore articles, and stay updated on library timings all from anywhere, anytime. This platform brings the campus library closer to every student.
